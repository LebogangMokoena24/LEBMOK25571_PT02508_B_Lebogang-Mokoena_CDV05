About Me

Where finance meets technology.

I have always been drawn to two things: how money moves and how technology enables that movement. Finance is about understanding value and how it is created, measured, and transferred. Technology is the infrastructure that makes that possible at scale. For me the two are inseparable.

That belief is what led me to study Business Administration at postgraduate level, pursue the CFA Programme, and simultaneously sharpen my coding skills at CodeSpace Academy. I am not choosing between finance and tech. I am deliberately building fluency in both because the most impactful professionals in fintech can speak both languages.

I am someone who leans into learning. Whether it is a new JavaScript framework, a new financial concept, or a new way of thinking about a problem, I show up with curiosity and follow through with commitment. I like building things that are clean, purposeful, and work well in code as much as in strategy.
